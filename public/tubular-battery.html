Tubular Batteries

We have gained immense admiration from our respected clients for manufacturing and supplying premium quality Tubular Batteries. These are lead-acid batteries in which positive plate is replaced by a tube made of cloth and coated well. Our batteries are used in applications including home power backup, electric propulsion of vehicles, and solar equipment. They are known to last twice than normal batteries due to their specific design. Tabular Batteries have a life expectancy of up to 5 years.
